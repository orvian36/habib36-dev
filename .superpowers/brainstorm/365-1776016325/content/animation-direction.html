Which animation personality fits habib36.dev?

This sets the tone for all transitions, hovers, and scroll effects across the site

A

Cinematic & Orchestrated

Staggered reveals that feel like a choreographed sequence. Elements slide, fade, and scale in with precise timing — like a film title sequence. Hero text letter-by-letter animation, sections that build themselves as you scroll. High impact, higher complexity.

Think: Apple product pages, Stripe homepage, Linear.app
B

Subtle & Buttery Smooth

Refined micro-interactions that feel invisible but polished. Soft fade-ups on scroll, magnetic hover effects on cards, smooth page transitions, spring-based physics. Nothing screams "animated" — everything just feels right. Professional, fast-feeling.

Think: Vercel dashboard, Raycast, Notion transitions
C

Hacker Terminal Energy

Lean into the terminal aesthetic hard. Text that types itself everywhere, glitch effects on hover, matrix-rain particles, command-line-style page transitions ("> loading /projects..."), cursor blink animations. Memorable, on-brand, polarizing.

Think: Hackthebox, old-school terminal UIs, CLI aesthetics
D

Hybrid: B + C

Buttery smooth as the foundation (card hovers, scroll reveals, page transitions), with selective terminal touches where they reinforce the brand — hero typing effect, code-block animations, terminal-style stat counters. Best of both without going overboard.

Think: Brittany Chiang meets hacker aesthetic
